Yello Explains Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Yello Explains ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our services, including our website and TikTok content publishing integration.

1. Information We Collect

Yello Explains is an automated content publishing tool operated by a single developer. We do not collect personal information from end users. The only data processed is:

  • TikTok account credentials and OAuth tokens provided by the account owner (the developer) to authenticate with the TikTok API.
  • Video files, titles, and descriptions generated automatically for publishing purposes.

2. How We Use Information

The information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate with the TikTok Content Posting API on behalf of the authorized account owner.
  • Upload and publish short educational video content to the designated TikTok account.

We do not use any collected data for advertising, profiling, or any secondary purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any information to outside parties. Data is only transmitted to TikTok via their official API as necessary to publish content.

4. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with the TikTok for Developers API. Use of TikTok's platform is subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We do not store user data beyond what is necessary to complete individual API requests. OAuth tokens are stored locally by the developer and are not shared or persisted on any public server.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the credentials and tokens used to access TikTok's API. Access is limited to the account owner only.

7. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
